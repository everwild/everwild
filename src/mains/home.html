Why

一个从东京出发
通向山野的户外品牌

EVERWILD exists for people who want more than a one-off event. It is built for repeat returns: to train, to travel, to go outdoors with intention, and to keep moving with the right people.

Brand Statement

认真，不必拘谨。野一点，也可以有秩序。

The brand brings together mountain experiences, structured coaching, recurring community, and media that keeps the story alive long after the day is over.

Principle 01

从城市出发，但不止于城市。

Training in Tokyo is not the end goal. It is the base camp for bigger terrain, longer efforts, and more expansive weekends.

Principle 02

既欢迎第一次，也服务长期投入。

Beginners need clarity. Experienced athletes need structure. The best brands create room for both at the same time.

Principle 03

活动、训练、社群和内容不是分开的。

Each part strengthens the others. Better training leads to better trips. Better stories create stronger trust. Better community keeps people returning.
